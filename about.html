About Us

The story of Taste of Nepal and our passion for authentic Nepali cuisine

Our Story

Taste of Nepal was founded in 2010 by Chef Rajesh Sharma, who grew up in the foothills of the Himalayas. After moving abroad, he missed the authentic flavors of his homeland and decided to bring the rich culinary traditions of Nepal to food enthusiasts around the world.

What started as a small family-run restaurant has now grown into a beloved culinary destination, known for its authentic Nepali dishes and warm hospitality. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the true essence of Nepali cuisine.

At Taste of Nepal, we believe that food is not just about nourishment, but also about culture, tradition, and bringing people together. Every dish we serve tells a story of Nepal's rich heritage and diverse culinary landscape.

Our Heritage & Culture

Nepali cuisine is as diverse as its landscape, influenced by its neighbors India, Tibet, and China, yet maintaining its unique identity. The food varies from region to region, reflecting the country's geographical diversity and cultural richness.

Traditional Nepali cooking methods and spices are at the heart of our kitchen. We use authentic ingredients like jimbu (Himalayan herb), timur (Nepali pepper), and a variety of lentils and grains that are staples in Nepali households.

Our restaurant's decor is inspired by traditional Nepali architecture and art, featuring hand-carved wooden elements, colorful prayer flags, and thangka paintings. We strive to create an immersive experience that transports you to Nepal.
